About Our Company

Quality and Affordable Logistics Company

Al-Gammal Transport and Logistics Company, affiliated with the Al-Gammal Group of Companies for General Contracting, was established in 2018. It operates in various transportation service activities, boasting a fleet of trucks, excavators, private cars, and multiple buses. This enables the company to meet the transportation needs of equipment and heavy loads across the Republic of Egypt.

Additionally,

Al-Gammal has a specialized department for welding polyethylene pipes, catering to major projects throughout the Arab Republic of Egypt.
Fleet

Ability to deliver goods quickly.

Al-Gammal Transport and Logistics Company operates a versatile fleet of trucks tailored to meet various customer needs. This includes the transportation of polyethylene and plastic pipes, as well as trucks specialized for delivering food, mineral water, and other goods.
